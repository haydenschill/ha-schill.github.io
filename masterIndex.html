Hello!

I'm a PhD candidate at the University of California, San Diego, working in Dr. Timothy Brady's Vision and Memory Lab. Before starting at UCSD, I was a research assistant in Dr. Jeremy Wolfe's Visual Attention Lab, and received my undergraduate BS in Neuroscience working with Dr. Jason Haberman on the capacity limitations of Ensemble Perception.

I am interested in investigating the structure of memory broadly, as well as the applied nature of psychology research, namely in the field of radiology.

You can see a broader overview of my research projects here. I have also included a list of my publications and presentations for those interested, as well as a descritiopn of my teaching and mentorship experience. Here is my CV available to download.

If you'd like to contact me, please don't hesitate to email me!
